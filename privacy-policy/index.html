Our Commitment to Your Privacy

GharSetu Realities ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. When you trust us with your details, we want you to be fully informed about how and why we use your data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us, which may include:

  • Your full name
  • Phone number and WhatsApp contact
  • Email address
  • Property requirements, budget range, and location preferences

We do not collect any sensitive financial information, government IDs, or payment details through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide real estate advisory services, including:

  • Responding to your property enquiries
  • Scheduling consultations and property walkthroughs
  • Sharing relevant property listings that match your requirements
  • Communicating updates about your transaction progress

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

Data Security

All client communications and personal data are treated with strict confidentiality. We use industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse. Our team members are bound by confidentiality obligations and access your data only as required to serve you.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Web3Forms to process contact form submissions securely. This service may temporarily store your submitted data to facilitate message delivery. We do not use advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or data brokers on this website.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request its correction or deletion, or withdraw your consent to us contacting you at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please reach out to us directly — we will respond promptly.
